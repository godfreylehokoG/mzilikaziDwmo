About Mzilikazi Heritage and History

King Mzilikazi was a significant figure in southern African history and is often regarded as one of the most prominent leaders of the Ndebele people. 

Early Life and Rise to Power:
Origins: Mzilikazi was born around 1790, a Khumalo by clan, in what is now South Africa. He initially served under the Zulu king Shaka but later broke away, leading his followers, the Khumalo clan, on a long migration northward.

Queen Cynthia Mzilikazi

Meet our Queen

Queen Cynthia Mzilikazi Khumalo stands as a beacon of leadership and cultural preservation in the Mzilikazi Royal Kingdom. As the current queen, she is dedicated to upholding the rich heritage of her ancestors while leading her people toward a future of progress and unity. Her reign is marked by a commitment to community empowerment, fostering partnerships, and ensuring that the legacy of the Mzilikazi Kingdom continues to inspire and uplift generations to come.
